Prakash Foundation

Empowering Talented and Underprivileged Girls Since 2012

Our Mission

Cultural traditions like dowries make a girl from a poor family become a burden. As a result, girls from poor families are less educated and have a high risk for early marriage and exploitation, such as child labor and prostitution. Providing a good education to talented and financially weak girls is the best solution to solve the problem.

Prakash Foundation, a NGO and a non-profit organization formed in 2012, provides scholarships to such girls studying in the 11th and 12th standard, irrespective of caste, community and religion, for higher professional studies in India. Prakash Foundation also arranges free coaching classes for selected girls to participate in Engineering and Medical entrance tests. The Prakash Foundation beneficiaries also take a pledge to support the Foundation after they start earning by sponsoring at least 2 girls.

Eligibility

Requirements to be eligible for a scholarship:

  • Must be a girl studying science in the 11th Standard
  • A score of above 80% in CBSC/ICSC Board
  • A score above 75% in the State Board Examination
  • Combined family income should be less than Rs. 100000/year
The scholarship will be renewed in the 12th standard based on the girl's performance.

Process

The President of the Prakash Foundation, with the help of other institutions and volunteers, will identify girls eligible for scholarships. Selected students will then fill up the required application forms. Filled up application forms along with other documents of these short-listed students are then sent to the donors to sponsor a student through the “Adopt a Scholar “ program. The sponsorship checks sent by the donors on the name of the beneficiaries are delivered to the girls by the President during an annual program organized by the Prakash Foundation.

The scholarship amount is Rs 10,000 or $175 per annum per student for 2 years, Inter 11th & 12th classes.

Application

In order to apply, the student will need to provide the following documents:

  • 10th Standard Marks Sheet
  • Bonafide certificate from school/college showing admission in Intermediate Science class.
  • Declaration of family occupation and income by parents/a certificate of income from a competent government authority.
  • Annual Expenditure of school fee, stationary etc. and to be approved by competent authority of school.
  • Receipt of money spent at the time of admission
  • Photocopy of bank passbook details of student
  • 1 Passport size photo of the student
